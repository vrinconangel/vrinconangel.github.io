OSU Cascades

OSU Cascades CS degree

OSU Cascades is located in Bend Oregon. the university offers a Computer science major with an option in web and mobile software development option. The requirements for the degree are 32-38 credits of Baccalaureate core, 50 credits of Pre-Computer Science, 47 of Pro-School Computer Science, 32 credits in the Applied Computer Science Option and electives if needed. Because most of todays society works and function on mobile application, this program may attract many students.
